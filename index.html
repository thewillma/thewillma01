About us

igniting a brigher future for the youth of Africa

The WillMa youth club promotes youth involvement and provides assistance, safe and accessible space for young people to socialise for personal and community development. We inspire and motivate young people to be confident and more active in their personal and community engagement. We also aim at building a unified network of young people who will act as volunteers in serving their communities and champion sustainable development through civic engagement.

OUR VISION STATEMENT

The club and its committee envision a community where young people will actively and voluntarily connect and participate in positive activities to create solutions to global issues that affects their everyday lives and the future.

OUR MISSION STATEMENT

The WillMa youth club and its committee aims at building bridges to inspire, empower and transform rural youth to be self-reliant and independent to develop ambitions for learning, relevant skills for work and healthy lifestyles.
