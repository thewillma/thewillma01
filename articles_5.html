EDUCATIONAL MATERIALS FOR BOLI JHS FINAL YEAR STUDENTS

Pens, Books, Mathematical sets and many more to ease burden on parents

We are committed to education and community development. Recently, we donated essential educational materials to 66 students of Boli M/A Junior High School as they prepare for the upcoming Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE). Our donation included mathematical sets and pens, aiming to provide these students with the tools they need to excel academically and approach their exams with confidence. But our contribution went beyond just providing materials; we also motivated the students to embrace learning, emphasizing the importance of thorough preparation and discouraging exam malpractice. We shared valuable tips and strategies to enhance their study habits, inspiring them to reach their full potential. This initiative highlights our commitment to bridging the gap in access to educational resources and fostering better learning opportunities. Our efforts were made possible through our collaboration with the Wa West and Lambussie CAMFED Association, demonstrating the power of partnership in making a positive difference in our community.
